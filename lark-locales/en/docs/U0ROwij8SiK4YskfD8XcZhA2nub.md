1. For user-submitted information that requires manual review, or when the Back Office manually adds a card on behalf of a user:

- That is, when the bank card verification status is "Pending Verification," manual review is required.

- When the user's bank card verification status is "Verified" or "Unverified," the backend may modify the bank card information.

1. Manually update the deposit verification status

- If the user's deposit has completed but the card's verification status has not been updated to normal, you can manually handle this via [Update Deposit Verification Status].

<img src="/assets/EioAbQ4YKoWJXNx5wBsjOMH2pnc.png" src-width="2862" src-height="800" align="center"/>

- After associating the customer's deposit details, or even without associating a deposit record, the system will automatically set the deposit verification status to "Witnessed".
