# 6.0.5 Callable Bull/Bear Contracts / Equity Recovery Operation (TM/DS Category)

# 6.0.5 Callable Bull/Bear Contracts / Equity Recall Operation (TM/DS Categories)

a. On the "Corporate Actions" page, click "New" to add a corporate action.

<img src="/assets/VdWBbw3ePoqr9ExN8ZwjqI3ZpPe.png" src-width="2628" src-height="1231" align="center"/>

b. Select manual creation: choose the Announcement Type (see 5.1 Announcement Types for reference): TM (Take-Over (Compulsory)) / Target (stock code) / enter an Announcement Number (must be unique) / select Ex-dividend Date or Record Date, then click "Confirm".

Quick Create: only applicable when a CCNAN05 file has been imported.

<img src="/assets/WrX6bfoDmonEG1xzZnpjvWzXpef.png" src-width="2246" src-height="1141" align="center"/>

c. After adding, the item will be displayed on the main panel. Click "Details" in the action column to fill in the information.

<img src="/assets/STiPbWFeuo2tq5xuJtvjR9hQpsg.png" src-width="2639" src-height="1039" align="center"/>

d. In the "Announcement Information" field, click "Edit"; fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory. After completing the input, click "Confirm".

- Payment Date: this is the system accounting date (viewable on the "Settlement Management &gt; End-of-Day Tasks" page; the accounting date shown below is January 30).

<img src="/assets/Px7sbZXv4oL0DuxR9RwjJ07DpSc.png" src-width="2384" src-height="1455" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/LjjCb2joao2peFxSPDWjetqipng.png" src-width="2386" src-height="1449" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/QyWubVNi7oTqNBxx1cbjF3nwpIh.png" src-width="793" src-height="200" align="center"/>

e. In the "Scheme Information" field, click "Edit" to modify the scheme type information.

<img src="/assets/Esv5bvMejobjYNxc9xKj2Ooapdf.png" src-width="2403" src-height="1503" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/JckFbE17iojl22xahVtjogqZpBd.png" src-width="2390" src-height="1446" align="center"/>

f. On the "Edit Scheme" page, fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory; the default scheme is "Yes". After completing the input, click "Confirm".

- Scheme Type: "TM-EXPIRED" indicates recall of Callable Bull/Bear Contracts;
- Execution Currency: the currency to be distributed;
- Payout Rate: enter the per-share return amount as a ratio; for example, entering 0.78 = $0.78 net value returned per share. If there is no net residual value, enter 0.

<img src="/assets/M3pvb2DF6o7DsFx43ajj2nslpEd.png" src-width="1849" src-height="1442" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/TPyXbEqKEolP7SxpGPDjXHKVpPc.png" src-width="1667" src-height="1442" align="center"/>

g. In the "Fee Rules" field, review the fees charged. To modify the fees in a single operation, click "Edit"; after completing changes, click "Confirm".

<img src="/assets/UGW3bB3Lqo7lOQx0UhjjvE5opgd.png" src-width="2384" src-height="1449" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/N6DqbUKrLoZRdjxbmCDjLmxQprc.png" src-width="1843" src-height="1448" align="center"/>

h. If the Announcement Information, Scheme Information, and Fee Rules are correct, click "Register" and then "Confirm" to submit the registration.

<img src="/assets/PyHabVRSToNGovxWcoij918cpwg.png" src-width="2385" src-height="1445" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Q7CRbTa0AoShDSxjX8cjgL00pDc.png" src-width="2378" src-height="1442" align="center"/>

i. After "Register" succeeds, the transferred amount will be visible in the "Details Summary" field; if there is a discrepancy in the amount, click "Residual Adjustment" to modify the distribution amount.

<img src="/assets/CT4Kburu3oOazGxWRSujgn9cp3W.png" src-width="2389" src-height="1458" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/FK97bTqFnozh1GxCKKZjCatIpNf.png" src-width="2406" src-height="1263" align="center"/>

j. In the "Customer Details" field, view eligible equity clients.

- Click "Add": to add individual clients to the list;
- Click "Void": to remove a client;
- Click "Details": to modify an individual client's fee information or the distribution amount of their entitlement.

<img src="/assets/OSyqbyBE9oqM12x5c1MjdZdnp8d.png" src-width="2398" src-height="1497" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/IRfrbr8kzogYICx0bdBjXJZvpKe.png" src-width="1847" src-height="1438" align="center"/>

k. If any information has been modified, click "Re-register" to refresh the data. After confirming the information is correct, click "Submit for Execution" to proceed to the next step.

<img src="/assets/GfA1bCf6uovuJjxuOEkjp5e0p2d.png" src-width="2380" src-height="1448" align="center"/>

l. After confirming the total distribution amount and the total fees collected, click "Submit for Execution".

<img src="/assets/Lt5bbMBwuoujDAxnvDtjSq9ppZg.png" src-width="1678" src-height="1462" align="center"/>

m. The reviewer can click "Download Report SDR040-01" and select the PDF file, then view the corporate action details in "Report Management &gt; Export List." If the information is correct, click "Execute Review."

<img src="/assets/SYqXbejUloo8l2xvxEBjGhGmp5c.png" src-width="2633" src-height="1500" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/FEOfb9ImKoii1ExwIJgjTZTLpYb.png" src-width="1351" src-height="634" align="center"/>

n. After verifying the information, click "Approve." If checked, the app will send a push notification.

<img src="/assets/TD7NbKmI5oOgwQxy1knjxI4SpSc.png" src-width="2394" src-height="1453" align="center"/>

o. The status will be updated to "Execution Successful."

<img src="/assets/A6PhbNbrjomhwsx2Onzj01l3p1f.png" src-width="2386" src-height="286" align="center"/>

p. Records can be reviewed in "Assets Account &gt; Account Details &gt; Stocks" to view the transaction history.

<img src="/assets/ONDnbyj0Hohsi5x5W08jjpUdprb.png" src-width="2637" src-height="375" align="center"/>
