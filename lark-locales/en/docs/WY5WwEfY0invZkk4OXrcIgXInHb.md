# 7.1 Financing Interest

# 7.1A Q: How can financing interest be viewed or waived?

<b>A:</b> On the "Clearing Management &gt; Securities Lending Management &gt; Financing Interest Statement" page, query the customer's financing interest by account number.

<img src="/assets/NT7Cb9FnLoVDLJx4EoWjRmRUpeg.png" src-width="2154" src-height="1360" align="center"/>

# 7.1B Q: How can the interest be adjusted to the current day?

A: There is no function to adjust financing interest to the current day. You must perform the end-of-day "Funds Settlement" process on the following day before the interest can be adjusted to reflect today.

# 7.1C Q: How can interest be issued to customers in advance?

<b>A:</b> In "Clearing Management &gt; Financing Management," select "Deposit Interest / Financing Interest" and click "Early Settlement" to issue interest early or collect outstanding financing interest.
