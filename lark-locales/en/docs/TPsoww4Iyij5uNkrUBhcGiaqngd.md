# Global Search

Whale provides two Global Search tabs: <b>Search Jobs</b> and <b>Search Clients</b>.

<img src="/assets/WlYBbMD5OoTuLnxobYzjAyUJpxg.png" src-width="3112" src-height="758" align="center"/>

After signing in, a search button icon is available in the upper-right corner; the feature can also be activated using keyboard shortcuts.

<img src="/assets/X43nbcBNrovWQCx1iI2jiHLPp5f.png" src-width="3810" src-height="130" align="center"/>

## Keyboard Shortcuts

## Global Function Search

The Search Jobs tab allows you to enter relevant keywords to retrieve job functions, facilitating quick switching to the desired job.

<img src="/assets/ToOHbqESroAFFIx3oL9jVCMqp1P.png" src-width="2570" src-height="1428" align="center"/>

## Global Client Search

You can search client information by different fields.

<img src="/assets/Owt4bOk23oj3bNxvb3Ajfrf5pih.png" src-width="2570" src-height="1428" align="center"/>

After locating a client, a brief summary of the client's basic information (account contact information) will appear. The search supports:

- Client search supports fuzzy queries by name and securities account number.
- Name searches accept the client's English name and can display the client's English name.

<img src="/assets/EyVYbCcUCoD5T8xnJpfjeSpSprc.png" src-width="2608" src-height="1460" align="center"/>

After selecting a client record, a client summary and key asset transaction records will slide in from the left.

<img src="/assets/QfRdbvD0ooa8cDxyT7ujr8FKp9c.png" src-width="3800" src-height="1758" align="center"/>

<b>Asset Overview tab</b>: You can switch between different asset currencies to view the total asset amounts. The top-right area also provides an asset data dashboard.

Risk status and a financing summary are displayed in the area below.

<b>Cash Details</b>: Displays available cash and settlements pending delivery in different currencies.

<b>Position Details</b>: Displays holdings records such as stocks, options, and funds.

<img src="/assets/LwC9bbLDhoscvwxu8IfjdZp0pg5.png" src-width="3822" src-height="1914" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/NjrgbiqcxoVBNmx6r9Ij28g9pcf.png" src-width="1584" src-height="1834" align="center"/>

<b>Deposits & Withdrawals tab</b>: You can query the client's deposit and withdrawal records and directly edit them.

<img src="/assets/ZgFFbELvrom29YxqcqHjbt8opMP.png" src-width="1540" src-height="1616" align="center"/>

<b>Bank Card tab</b>: You can query the client's bank card information and directly edit it.

<img src="/assets/TccObNWE4ojEgoxAXbTjQEKApCf.png" src-width="1578" src-height="1290" align="center"/>

<b>Billing tab</b>: You can view the fee items related to the client's account.

<img src="/assets/Rh5kbUxNAoqYr6xo438jbtCcpog.png" src-width="1550" src-height="1182" align="center"/>
