# Quick Start

# Overview

The Whale KYC system supports handling customer account opening information-related workflows, including account opening review, account information modification, and other workflow management.

## Account Opening Review

<b>Step 1: View the Account Opening List</b>

1. When a customer submits an account opening application online, or when back-office personnel submits an application offline, the system will record it in the "Accounts" and set the status to "Pending Review".
2. Click the "Details" button to navigate to the full account information detail page.

<img src="/assets/KEQEbSgD3o4FOfx6ivTj3vBppvd.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

<b>Step 2: View Account Information</b>

On the "Account Details" page, all account information submitted by the customer is displayed.

<img src="/assets/R3jhb02H4oeRiDxzAshjsJf3pMd.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

<b>Step 3: Review Account Information</b>

In accordance with regulatory requirements and risk management policies, review the account information submitted by the customer as prescribed, including identity verification, address verification, financial information authenticity checks, and risk assessment.

1. Current step approved:

<img src="/assets/YVISbXehiok0PJxkOuzj7JG8prc.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

1. Current step rejected

Select the rejection reason and rejection page to return the application to the customer; the customer may update the information and resubmit the application.

<img src="/assets/JGChbyfw3od4mSxhTaojYb5Rpje.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

<b>Step 4: Account Activation</b>

If all review steps are approved, a securities account will be generated and the account will be successfully activated. The customer may then proceed with deposit and trading processes.

<img src="/assets/NuThbgbpCopLQix6F2QjiXTGpMe.png" src-width="2922" src-height="1456" align="center"/>

## Account Information Modification

<b>Step 1: Locate the Field to Modify</b>

Hover the cursor over the field and click the "Edit" button.

<img src="/assets/Z3hBb5z73o9hgkx9vKQjzdWHpKf.png" src-width="2922" src-height="1420" align="center"/>

<b>Step 2: Submit the Update</b>

In the update dialog, enter or select the new information and submit.

<img src="/assets/ORFTbArtso5kp5xmvFgjBhnZpie.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

<b>Step 3: Review the Modification Work Order</b>

On the work order approval page, find the current work order, then click "Approve" or "Reject" after confirmation.

If you click "Approve," the information modification will succeed; refresh the account details page to display the updated data.

If you click "Reject," the information modification request will not be approved.

<img src="/assets/QD58bWsKcop6iZxEQHYjcf7gpAd.png" src-width="1870" src-height="1308" align="center"/>

## Account Information Entry

<img src="/assets/IqDJboW2Wo4yDhxt1WvjEtK1p1d.png" src-width="2986" src-height="1404" align="center"/>

For this task, directly enter the customer's application information. Click "Add Account Application" at the top right to enter the account opening page as shown below:

<img src="/assets/T81sbHF8FokKaKxrRTkjnrs6p5g.png" src-width="2466" src-height="1042" align="center"/>

### Step 1: Select Account Opening Type

The account opening process is divided into three steps: select an existing customer or create a new customer, choose the customer type, and select the customer's nationality.

Select Existing Customer / Create New Customer

Select Existing Customer: Choose a registered customer (APP user). You may search by customer number, phone number, email, full name, or identity card.

<img src="/assets/N5rUb3LVmou2xXxl7gnjEPHLp8d.png" src-width="2488" src-height="1370" align="center"/>

Create New Customer: For customers not registered in the system, you may create them through this task. The system currently offers multiple login methods to choose from:

- System-generated APP account login
- Mobile phone verification code login
- Email verification login

<img src="/assets/Cvllb7JRYovwnbx2poxjEAMXpCg.png" src-width="2514" src-height="1426" align="center"/>

Select Customer Type: Options include Individual Customer / Joint Account Customer / Corporate Customer.

<img src="/assets/WGbEbZsGSoKmMmxa2h1jNWFFppg.png" src-width="2506" src-height="1350" align="center"/>

Note: For corporate accounts, you may modify the default CRS corporate entity type for the company account.

<img src="/assets/CYf3bBnndo8Mx8xJjJnjvWBApKw.png" src-width="2474" src-height="1546" align="center"/>

### Step 2: Upload Identity Documents

Customers are categorized into three nationality types:

1. Nationality: Mainland China
    1. Mainland China users must upload Mainland China identity card.
    2. Possession of a Hong Kong identity document: default is "No"; if the user selects "Yes," guide the user to provide supplementary Hong Kong document information.

2. Nationality: Hong Kong, China
    1. Hong Kong users must upload Hong Kong identity card and fill in corresponding information.

3. Nationality: Other
    1. Other nationality users must upload identity document(s) and passport.
    2. Possession of a Hong Kong identity document: default is "No"; if the user selects "Yes," guide the user to provide supplementary Hong Kong document information.
    3. If the user selects "No," upload other identity documents.

<img src="/assets/RrIMbiizZo23Oax2TVyjZ5QYppc.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

### Step 3: Upload Proof of Address

Customers are categorized into two types:

1. Mainland China nationality users
    1. May use identity card as proof of residential address.
    2. If address proof document type is selected as "Identity Card," no residential address proof upload is required.
    3. Address proof document type selection:

2. Others
    1. Must upload proof of residential address.

<img src="/assets/PLESbg1broctzgxSZfcjBSrKpMg.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

### Step 4: Complete Various Basic Information

This primarily includes occupational information, tax information, email address, and other details. Among them:

Tax Information: Enter the country/region where the user is taxed and the tax identification number. Up to 10 entries are supported. Fields include: Tax Country/Region and Taxpayer Identification Number.

<img src="/assets/UjlCbQBChopwxpxaJ2gjqJiHpgb.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

Email Address: The email used by the user to receive statements, notifications, and other notices. Each APP user must have a unique email address.

Enter contact phone number. Multiple customers may use the same contact phone number.

<img src="/assets/NmXcbPjEzoSOrlxZnRIjUlQapqd.png" src-width="2472" src-height="1436" align="center"/>

### Step 7: Confirm Information

The system will display the customer information arranged by sections such as: identity documents / personal basic information / occupational information / asset and investment information / compliance confirmation / linked account information, making it easy to review and confirm the entered information.

<img src="/assets/C8dOb8najohRarxxn97jDRumpLd.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

### Step 8: Select Account Type and Upload Account Opening Documents

Upload account opening documents and custom documents:

1. Base documents are system-generated account opening related files; information entered in the back office is auto-filled.
2. Business staff may print the documents for the customer to sign.

<img src="/assets/BKDvb3P4Yo1kSRxGI66jfTGfpTc.png" src-width="3024" src-height="1648" align="center"/>

Account Type: You may choose to open a margin account or a cash account; the system supports opening multiple accounts simultaneously.

<img src="/assets/OU5ebvcROollTAxTCpYjihhwprg.png" src-width="2426" src-height="900" align="center"/>

Other configuration information includes: whether to receive offline statements / broker / witness employee name / license number / securities number (custom input for customer account) / customer declaration and information confirmation.
