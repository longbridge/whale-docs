# 6.0.3 Rights Issue / Tender Offer Operations (RS/TU/TC/OO Categories)

# 6.0.3 Rights/Take-Over Operations (RS/TU/TC/OO Categories)

a. On the "Corporate Actions" page, click "New" to add a corporate action.

<img src="/assets/LiGib0YghoQHdQxosPhjgrXwpBg.png" src-width="2628" src-height="1231" align="center"/>

b. Select Manual Create: choose the Announcement Type (refer to 5.1 Announcement Types: RS (Right Subscription), OO (Open Offer), TU (Take-Over (Unconditional)), TC (Take-Over (Conditional))) / Underlying (stock code) / enter Announcement Number (must be unique) / select Ex-Date or Record Date, then click "Confirm".

Quick Create: only applicable when a CCNAN05 file has been imported.

The following uses RS (Right Subscription) as an example.

<img src="/assets/J8dfbugqHovs6Hx7848jAFbYpxK.png" src-width="2246" src-height="1141" align="center"/>

c. After adding, the item will appear on the main panel. Click "Details" in the action column to complete the information.

<img src="/assets/Qaf7bGWqHoneJ4xWKvzjgUlHpIf.png" src-width="2641" src-height="798" align="center"/>

d. In the "Announcement Information" section, click "Edit"; fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory. After completing the entries, click "Confirm".

- Distribution: generally NOMINEE
- Select Start Time: the start time chosen by the client in the app
- Select End Time: the end time chosen by the client in the app
- Select End Time (CCASS): the CCASS end time
- Payment Date: the system accounting date (viewable on the "Clearing Management &gt; End-of-Day Tasks" page; the accounting date shown below is February 2)

Note*: The app currently does not support clients selecting corporate actions of the RS, OO, TU, or TC types.

<img src="/assets/WFGIb7kwhoOrSCx6rBmjISWjpWb.png" src-width="2308" src-height="607" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/KQXNbP66soPKyux4Uenjm6MspHe.png" src-width="2390" src-height="1399" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/T2qubyQ2rovxoAxa1SQjM5dKp6e.png" src-width="719" src-height="183" align="center"/>

e. In the "Plan Information" section, click "Edit" to modify the rights distribution details.

<img src="/assets/JHVEbUTHCobZZOxEobGjNW4Ppi5.png" src-width="2385" src-height="1391" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/FavIbwZ55o1WYGxktetjLTGTpvD.png" src-width="1848" src-height="1386" align="center"/>

f. On the "Edit Plan" page, fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory. The default plan is "Yes." After completing entries, click "Confirm".

- Plan Type: "RS-Subscription" denotes a rights distribution;
- Execution Currency: the currency of distribution
- Distribution Instrument: the stock code of the distributed security
- Reinvestment Price: the price of the distributed rights

<img src="/assets/UL2Jb3aSBowyuGxzRjmjfm3Opyg.png" src-width="1851" src-height="1390" align="center"/>

g. Mandatory fields for announcement types RS, OO, TU, and TC are listed below:

Note* RS - Rights Subscription (exercise of rights); OO - Open Offer (exercise of open offer); TU - Take-Over (Unconditional) (full takeover); TC - Take-Over (Conditional) (partial takeover)

The operation flow is consistent across types.

(i). Plan Type:

RS - Rights Subscription (exercise of rights)

- Execution Currency: the currency of distribution
- Distribution Instrument: the stock code of the distributed security
- Reinvestment Price: the price of the distributed rights

<img src="/assets/Ngotb73l0oCDamxVwQgjTMUapSh.png" src-width="471" src-height="349" align="center"/>

(ii). Plan Type:

OO - Open Offer (exercise of open offer)

- Execution Currency: the currency of distribution
- Distribution Instrument: the stock code of the distributed security
- Reinvestment Price: the price of the distributed shares

<img src="/assets/K6chbLpBYoFeIqx6L19j2mSipEd.png" src-width="473" src-height="348" align="center"/>

(iii). Plan Type:

TU - Take-Over (Unconditional) (full takeover)

- Execution Currency: the currency of distribution
- Reinvestment Price: the offer price for the shares

<img src="/assets/BKZib7499ons0Vxd5FSj2PoUpXd.png" src-width="472" src-height="364" align="center"/>

(iv). Plan Type:

TC - Take-Over (Conditional) (partial takeover)

- Execution Currency: the currency of distribution
- Reinvestment Price: the offer price for the shares

<img src="/assets/TLtXbNZvFodLgExhXDcjUdAmpoi.png" src-width="471" src-height="361" align="center"/>

h. In the "Fee Rules" section, view the charged fees. To modify fees as a one-time change, click "Edit". After completing modifications, click "Confirm".

<img src="/assets/IU78be5OhomseZxoEHTj9GaHp0d.png" src-width="2361" src-height="1385" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/LsT1bXCNBoOepexta7wjOPGtp9b.png" src-width="1831" src-height="1386" align="center"/>

i. After verifying the Announcement Information, Plan Information, and Fee Rules, click "Register" and then "Confirm" to submit the registration.

<img src="/assets/BAwNbHXLioIiQyx8FeFj8pYmpsc.png" src-width="2369" src-height="1388" align="center"/>

Note* The app has not yet integrated the client-side option to select rights distribution features.

<img src="/assets/NCXebG6Z9ol8vHx6wMUjlWJGpDe.png" src-width="2369" src-height="1388" align="center"/>

j. After successful registration, the "Details Summary" section will display exercisable shares and the number of clients.

<img src="/assets/Gzdhb9fLPohEVuxzkKHjuBPjpGb.png" src-width="2367" src-height="1392" align="center"/>

k. In the "Client Details" section, view the eligible distribution clients.
Click "Add": to add individual clients to the distribution list;
Click "Void": to remove a client;
Click "Details": to modify an individual client's fee information or the transfer portion of their entitlement information.

<img src="/assets/B6GdboCV0ou1Jlxgy3YjjiGjpYd.png" src-width="2391" src-height="1438" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/McvxbWX2noF6Ayxuc9VjMRFspsc.png" src-width="1849" src-height="1390" align="center"/>

l. If any information has been modified, click "Re-register" to refresh the data. After confirming the data is correct, click "Collect Exercise Instructions" to proceed to the next step.

Time periods must progress sequentially: select Start Time &lt; End Time &lt; End Time (CCASS)

<img src="/assets/WFvQbZGOrof1ZbxgLj8jcVT2pMX.png" src-width="2393" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

Tick whether to notify clients, then click "Confirm"; the status will update to "Collecting Instructions".

Note* The app has not yet integrated the client-side option to select rights distribution features.

<img src="/assets/GRh9b1wv3okQe4x0j9Mjo2kFpCw.png" src-width="2036" src-height="1182" align="center"/>

m. While in the "Collecting Instructions" status, you may edit the number of shares to be exercised for clients. In the "Client Details" section, click "Details".

<img src="/assets/TPtcbeNnCo9trsxt3BQjaSmRpKc.png" src-width="2620" src-height="1492" align="center"/>

n. Click "Exercise" to modify the actual number of shares the client will exercise.

Please ensure the client has sufficient funds in the account reserved for subscription.

<img src="/assets/RvCmb2QQso7VO3xda7UjlL5wpCh.png" src-width="2353" src-height="1412" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/YpaQbyn92oFxIhxlTp8j8PMbp74.png" src-width="2350" src-height="1178" align="center"/>

o. If you need to change a client's registered exercise quantity, first click "Revoke" under "Other Information (rights, offers, etc.)", and then click "Exercise" to modify the quantity.

<img src="/assets/RTRXbzcNFoyT6IxpQVuj1C1cpwb.png" src-width="2865" src-height="1619" align="center"/>

p. After confirming the client's exercise quantities, you may click "Complete Instruction Collection Early".

<img src="/assets/PeQQbXVBYojYKdx0MKRjdqmfpmc.png" src-width="2868" src-height="1679" align="center"/>

q. Then click "Report Exercise Instructions".

Select whether to send an in-app push notification to clients.

<img src="/assets/H6bebWaj2o6hWkxM89Qjy0lEpMe.png" src-width="2630" src-height="1495" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/CTvTbRMbbovZBOxmqxfjIySPpPZ.png" src-width="2634" src-height="1488" align="center"/>

r. Click "Backfill" to confirm exercise quantities; you may choose Original Value Backfill or Sub-Account Backfill and enter quantities, then click "Confirm".

Note*: The Backfill operation refers to the action of importing the final CCASS processing results back into the system after selected client instructions have been uploaded to CCASS for processing.

<img src="/assets/VDRWbpgUqo0g10xJyaqjnLpcpxh.png" src-width="2228" src-height="1419" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Qa7gbu7HhoW17qxbYfijqR1BpZf.png" src-width="2084" src-height="1404" align="center"/>

s. After verifying all information is correct, click "Submit for Execution".

<img src="/assets/EeN2bx97fokpfOxhmhmj2Lj4pAg.png" src-width="2626" src-height="1498" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/M2OTbfmcIoIaZFxjHc5joe4opOc.png" src-width="2277" src-height="1487" align="center"/>

t. Review personnel may click "Download Report SDR040-03" and select the PDF file; view the corporate action details under "Report Management &gt; Export List". If the data is correct, click "Perform Review".

<img src="/assets/RaJZbbzuyo4P41x6hdNj6LKkpZf.png" src-width="2631" src-height="1495" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/WSDybezEtovjwMxYCbWjZblRpUc.png" src-width="1600" src-height="1124" align="center"/>

u. After verifying the information, click "Approve". If selected, the app will send a push notification.

<img src="/assets/UuibbHCK1o8cc6xGiKLjaDjOpAd.png" src-width="2255" src-height="1430" align="center"/>

v. The status will update to "Execution Successful".

<img src="/assets/XCk2bP8AuoLW1MxzXytjIrMMpsL.png" src-width="1972" src-height="1437" align="center"/>

x. To review distribution records, check the transaction history at "Assets Account &gt; Account Details &gt; Stocks".

<img src="/assets/IMmdbydlXoWHLTxrZuLjdCK0pyg.png" src-width="2868" src-height="424" align="center"/>
