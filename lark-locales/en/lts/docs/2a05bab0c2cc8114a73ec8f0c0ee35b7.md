# Overview

Depositing cryptocurrency refers to the process of transferring cryptocurrency (such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.) from one address (for example, another exchange or another person's wallet) to one‚Äôs own digital wallet or trading platform account. This process relies on blockchain technology to ensure the secure transfer of assets, and requires wallet address verification to ensure operational compliance and the security of funds.

# Deposit Procedure

1. Open the target platform/wallet and navigate to the "Top-up / Deposit" page.
2. Select the corresponding cryptocurrency and network (must match the sender).
3. Copy the generated deposit address (verify it is correct).
4. Log in to the sender‚Äôs platform, paste the address, enter the amount, and submit after completing the security verification.
5. Wait for block confirmations, then check the deposit status on the target platform/wallet.
