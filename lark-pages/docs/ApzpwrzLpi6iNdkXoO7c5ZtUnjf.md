# Announcement Management

# Announcement Management

Announcement Management is primarily designed to enable brokerage operations personnel to issue system-related notices to customers via trading terminals such as the App. Such announcements may include the following:

- Brokerage business explanations
- Notifications of system service changes
- System-related upgrade notices

Through the operational management of the global announcement module, brokerage firms can reduce the manual processing of customer notices and improve the timeliness and efficiency of announcement management.

# Applicable Scenarios

This operation is performed when brokerage operations personnel need to manage external announcements (Create / Edit / Disable).

# Prerequisites

None

# Operating Instructions

Menu path: Announcement Management &gt; Announcement Management

## Announcement Management

This operation is primarily used for maintaining system announcements directed at customer-facing terminals.

Supported markets:

- For the announcement types General Announcement, Regular Market Closure, Temporary Market Closure, and Channel Issue, market selection is supported.
