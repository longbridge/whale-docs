# 6.0.2 Share Split/Consolidation and Share Conversion Operations (CA Category)

# 6.0.2 Share Split/Consolidation and Conversion Operations (CA Category)

a. On the "Corporate Actions" page, click "New" to add a corporate action.

<img src="/assets/Dt6HbhUimoHtlcxwKG8jhATcpfc.png" src-width="2628" src-height="1231" align="center"/>

b. Select "Create Manually": choose the Announcement Type (refer to 5.1 Announcement Types: CA (Corporate Action) / Underlying (Stock Code) / enter an Announcement Number (must be unique) / select Ex-Date or Record Date), then click "Confirm".

Quick Create: only applicable if a CCNAN05 file has been imported.

<img src="/assets/KTHUbpP5LobwEYxUPXnjEnqip4f.png" src-width="2246" src-height="1141" align="center"/>

c. After addition is complete, the item will display on the main interface. Click "Details" in the Actions column to fill in the information.

<img src="/assets/Mt9Wbxav9oUj2LxlTaLjSGeNpme.png" src-width="2638" src-height="980" align="center"/>

d. In the "Announcement Information" section, click "Edit"; fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required. After completing the inputs, click "Confirm".

- Payment Date: the system accounting date (can be viewed on the Clearing Management &gt; End-of-Day Tasks page; the accounting date shown below is February 2).

<img src="/assets/SpQ0b3oOwogu6AxI0Kvj3mrOpvh.png" src-width="2202" src-height="571" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Inp8bVINBoDzz1x9NPZj8kFNpTU.png" src-width="2306" src-height="1227" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Jh6XbC99loFvOWxw72GjY4GapHe.png" src-width="719" src-height="183" align="center"/>

e. In the "Scheme Information" section, click "Edit" to modify the share conversion details.

<img src="/assets/GcTybmeALovd8vx7RBYjo1mJpwf.png" src-width="2387" src-height="1436" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/OxplbDrGRonxlzxB1wQj4ViupCh.png" src-width="1844" src-height="1386" align="center"/>

f. On the "Edit Scheme" page, fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required. The default scheme is set to "Yes". After completing the inputs, click "Confirm".

- Scheme Type: "CA-Share Conversion" denotes share conversion.
- Distributed Security: the stock code to be distributed.

For example: every ten shares are converted into one share.

- Ratio (Old): Enter "10"
- Ratio (New): Enter "1"

<img src="/assets/ZT2LbtWVdoFnWCxqP4bjELXApu7.png" src-width="1849" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

g. Required fields for different CA announcement types are listed below:

Note* CA - Share Conversion, CA - Share Consolidation, and CA - Share Subdivision/Splitting require the same input fields and follow the same operational procedure.

(i). Type:

CA - Share Conversion

- Distributed Security (stock code to be distributed)

For example: every four shares are converted into one share.

- Ratio (Old): Enter "4"
- Ratio (New): Enter "1"

<img src="/assets/FyQ0bjyxSoXozYxGzBLj0QS7p7c.png" src-width="469" src-height="334" align="center"/>

(ii). Scheme Type:

CA - Share Consolidation

- Distributed Security (stock code to be distributed)

For example: every four shares are consolidated into one share.

- Ratio (Old): Enter "4"
- Ratio (New): Enter "1"

<img src="/assets/VRbnb9zrGojQVDxlizhjsrDtpNe.png" src-width="471" src-height="344" align="center"/>

(iii). Scheme Type:

CA - Share Subdivision/Splitting

- Distributed Security (stock code to be distributed)

For example: every four shares are split into one share.

- Ratio (Old): Enter "4"
- Ratio (New): Enter "1"

<img src="/assets/DmWTbNAMPoLJCzxFzBzjWWnhpNc.png" src-width="469" src-height="343" align="center"/>

h. In the "Fee Rules" section, view the fees to be charged. To modify the fees as a one-time change, click "Edit".

After completing modifications, click "Confirm".

<img src="/assets/Hi9xbYSlgo1oinxFVLDjCAkppGb.png" src-width="2388" src-height="1404" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Zza1bsU5YoV71gx9iSvjrXa8pOh.png" src-width="1829" src-height="1391" align="center"/>

i. If the Announcement Information, Scheme Information, and Fee Rules are correct, click "Register" and then "Confirm" to submit the registration.

<img src="/assets/UUwWbmNA9or7YmxSECqjdu2upYg.png" src-width="1671" src-height="1405" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Q85PbsF2XokksPxYBuyjtGYMp1e.png" src-width="2372" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

j. After "Registration" succeeds, the transferred shares will be displayed in the "Summary Details" section. If the share counts differ, click "Rounding Adjustment" to modify the distribution quantity.

<img src="/assets/QopzbyzWMo5JjLxaSdjjSxHdpVc.png" src-width="2385" src-height="1399" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Vdu5bUL9FoGbESxwd4rjnihMpwc.png" src-width="515" src-height="356" align="center"/>

k. In the "Customer Details" section, eligible recipients are listed.
Click "Add": to add individual customers to the distribution list;
Click "Void": to remove customers;
Click "Details": to modify an individual customer's fee information or the transferred entitlement quantity.

<img src="/assets/Cihibvn6No718ExgWZujspl2p1m.png" src-width="2379" src-height="1390" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/UawqbvpS9o4wG4xYjw9jGJk0pdg.png" src-width="1847" src-height="1388" align="center"/>

l. If any data has been modified, click "Re-register" to refresh the information. After confirming the information is correct, click "Submit for Execution" to proceed to the next step.

<img src="/assets/QvB5b2owDobYNPxEAOJjOiu0pYf.png" src-width="2383" src-height="1399" align="center"/>

m. After confirming the total number of distributed shares and the total fees charged, click "Submit for Execution".

<img src="/assets/In2ZbCziKolTPhx2PnMjyLtMpkh.png" src-width="1682" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

n. Review personnel can click "Download Report SDR040-11" and select the PDF file, then view the details of this corporate action in Reports Management &gt; Export List. If the information is correct, click "Execute Review".

<img src="/assets/GlvPbF6aqo6IDvxtNcZj6uHDpue.png" src-width="2635" src-height="1401" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/BQoUbWd5Uo7KkYxMW84jTaB6p3f.png" src-width="1592" src-height="678" align="center"/>

o. Click "Approve". If selected, the app will send a push notification.

<img src="/assets/D48fbO3cEoiTl7xqK79jsJ9XpZd.png" src-width="2385" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

p. The status will update to "Execution Successful".

<img src="/assets/OFMQb83HIo5y1DxX6NMjWlCupvf.png" src-width="2353" src-height="997" align="center"/>

q. Transaction records can be reviewed under Assets Account &gt; Account Details &gt; Stocks to view the transaction history.

<img src="/assets/Ngjlb8JqBo9IvfxhdRajY3DUpVf.png" src-width="2626" src-height="376" align="center"/>
