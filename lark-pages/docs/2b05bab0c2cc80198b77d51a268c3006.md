    - Note: Virtual coin accounts are recorded using a combined instrument of "cryptocurrency + pricing currency": deposits and withdrawals are recorded per cryptocurrency, and when ultimately synchronized to the securities account, the combined instrument is used for posting (for example: cryptocurrency ETH with pricing currency USD will be posted as "VA/HAS/ETHUSD"). When debiting out, the system will preferentially deduct from the currency with the larger available balance; if the balance is insufficient, it will automatically deduct from the next priority currency.
