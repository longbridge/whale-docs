# Relationship Management

# Applicable Scenarios

Handles routine agent tasks related to client assignment and transfer management.

# Prerequisites

None

# Operating Instructions

Access the menu: Agent Management &gt; Relationship Management

## Agent Relationship Management

Allows querying the clients under a specific agent, or directly searching for an individual client.

<img src="/assets/AgLGbnV9XoQog8xTT8Ajwj0rpaf.png" src-width="3346" src-height="1104" align="center"/>

After locating a client, you can perform the actions [Terminate Agent Relationship] and [Transfer to Another Agent].

When changing a client's agent or assigning an agent, the system supports sending an optional email notification to the client.

<img src="/assets/NwBvbYyWxoeydtxo2eBjl24fpOc.png" src-width="3352" src-height="1362" align="center"/>
