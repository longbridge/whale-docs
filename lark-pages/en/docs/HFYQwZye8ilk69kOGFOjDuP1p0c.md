# 6.0.4 Additional Rights Issue / Public Offering Operations (ER/EO Category)

# 6.0.4 Excess Rights / Excess Open Offer Operations (ER/EO Category)

Note: If a client requires multiple subscriptions, create an ER/EO category for the additional subscription.

a. On the "Corporate Actions" page, click "New" to add a corporate action.

<img src="/assets/K7vUbCUOFooCfpxgHZdjkH4Rp6d.png" src-width="2628" src-height="1231" align="center"/>

b. Select manual creation: choose the announcement type (see 5.1 Announcement Types: ER (Excess Rights Subscription), EO (Excess Open Offer)) / Security (stock code) / enter the announcement number (must be unique) / select Ex-date or Record Date, then click "Confirm".

Quick create: only applicable when a CCNAN05 file has been imported.

The following example uses ER (Excess Rights Subscription).

<img src="/assets/HXCPbC9DlocCI3x5zUkjVkZTp3f.png" src-width="2246" src-height="1141" align="center"/>

c. After completion the entry will be displayed on the main interface. Click "Details" in the action column to fill in the information.

<img src="/assets/KsUpbVyV7olE4HxVUUgjQGz3pqe.png" src-width="2634" src-height="852" align="center"/>

d. In the "Announcement Information" section, click "Edit"; fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory. After entering the required information, click "Confirm".

- Distribution: generally NOMINEE
- Select Start Time: the time when clients begin making selections in the app
- Select End Time: the time when clients finish selections in the app
- Select End Time (CCASS): the CCASS end time
- Payment Date: the system accounting date (viewable on the "Clearing Management &gt; End-of-day Tasks" page; in the figure below the accounting date is January 30)

Note: The app currently does not support clients selecting ER/EO category corporate actions.

<img src="/assets/AzCubLF02o51IZxS0vwjDf8Xpdb.png" src-width="2388" src-height="670" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/CiXUbmYGroSQ5nxX31Ej8EJ3pzb.png" src-width="2389" src-height="1401" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/PWJNbmfVXoYwZvxsOQtjeJImpCe.png" src-width="793" src-height="200" align="center"/>

e. In the "Plan Information" section, click "Edit" to modify the subscription right details.

<img src="/assets/M4srbxvxtoN8h4xi0oUjUEb7plb.png" src-width="2384" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/O6nibvop3osNg2xJw5EjwFRdp7g.png" src-width="1852" src-height="1396" align="center"/>

f. On the "Edit Plan" page, fields marked with an asterisk (*) are mandatory. The default plan is "Yes". After entering the information, click "Confirm".

- Plan Type: "ER-Subscription" indicates an excess subscription;
- Settlement Currency: the currency to be distributed
- Allotted Security: the stock code to be distributed
- Reinvestment Price: the price for the distributed subscription right

<img src="/assets/KeI1bhVh9ovtwCxkRXgj6xo9psb.png" src-width="1849" src-height="1391" align="center"/>

g. Mandatory information for ER and EO announcement types is listed below:

Note: ER - Excess Rights Subscription (exercise of excess rights); EO - Excess Open Offer (exercise of excess open offer)

The input fields and operation flow are the same.

(i). Plan Type:

ER - Excess Rights Subscription (exercise of excess subscription rights)

- Settlement Currency: the currency to be distributed
- Allotted Security: the stock code to be distributed
- Reinvestment Price: the price for the distributed subscription right

<img src="/assets/IP8TbOmMUovFQpxDkyojcMClpuc.png" src-width="470" src-height="348" align="center"/>

(ii). Type:

EO - Excess Open Offer (exercise of excess open offer)

- Settlement Currency: the currency to be distributed
- Allotted Security: the stock code to be distributed
- Reinvestment Price: the price for the distributed subscription right

<img src="/assets/Iu5Jb3lifooSKTx6HrZjufDIpZt.png" src-width="475" src-height="342" align="center"/>

h. In the "Fee Rules" section, review the fees to be charged. To modify fees as a one-time change, click "Edit". After completing modifications, click "Confirm".

<img src="/assets/DpbBbQxr6ofCtlxWNs5jBXkjpPf.png" src-width="2366" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/V8dqbTZDlos3LZxRb8TjAJ4IpXU.png" src-width="1834" src-height="1380" align="center"/>

i. If the announcement information, plan information, and fee rules are correct, click "Register" and then "Confirm" to submit the registration.

<img src="/assets/C8mgblbLtoFmF2xwZPcjLcExp1c.png" src-width="2364" src-height="1391" align="center"/>

Note: The app has not yet integrated the feature that allows clients to select subscription rights.

<img src="/assets/FtI9bB36KobrRixnjP6jsDyWpQc.png" src-width="2372" src-height="1384" align="center"/>

j. After successful registration, the "Summary Details" section will display exercisable securities and the number of eligible clients.

<img src="/assets/A11ObRuhforeHUxDxQ4jZ97ep9f.png" src-width="2606" src-height="1580" align="center"/>

k. In the "Client Details" section you can view eligible distribution clients.

- Click "Add": to add individual clients to the distribution list;
- Click "Delete": to remove a client;
- Click "Details": to modify an individual client's fee information or the transfer allocation of entitlement information.

<img src="/assets/XZt4braS8orNepxry7kjpxKlpZc.png" src-width="2601" src-height="1579" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/MzN9b0GNDoODyDx3z8AjgZPmpOZ.png" src-width="1853" src-height="1388" align="center"/>

l. If any information has been modified, click "Re-register" to refresh the data. After confirming the information is correct, click "Collect Exercise Instructions" to proceed to the next step.

The time period must be sequential: Select Start Time &lt; Select End Time &lt; Select End Time (CCASS)

<img src="/assets/IvTVbBvkgo1bwoxvo3ijWdAypWK.png" src-width="2366" src-height="1395" align="center"/>

Check whether to notify clients, then click "Confirm"; the status will update to "Collecting Instructions".

Note: The app has not yet integrated the feature that allows clients to select subscription rights.

<img src="/assets/Fdz2bABgZoZzzRx4oFxjybL7pVu.png" src-width="2372" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

m. While in the "Collecting Instructions" status, you can edit the number of shares to be exercised for clients. In the "Client Details" section, click "Details".

<img src="/assets/GB5pbmgFpo0b8qxERqnj6zjTp8c.png" src-width="2372" src-height="390" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/FB78bNfVeowkkLxDvSojHJyepy8.png" src-width="2362" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

n. Click "Exercise" to modify the client's actual additional exercised share quantity.

Ensure the client has sufficient funds in their account to be frozen for the subscription.

<img src="/assets/C1fJbo9yRo1oNtx7Ps7jbYrJpEd.png" src-width="1850" src-height="1390" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/GTR6bOgiuoBPakxIg26j3HJXpTb.png" src-width="2381" src-height="1378" align="center"/>

o. If you need to modify a client's registered exercise quantity, first click "Revoke" in "Other Information (rights, offers, etc.)" and then click "Exercise" to change the quantity.

<img src="/assets/Z6PobvMxkoZUS2xWJukji80Xp3d.png" src-width="1859" src-height="1385" align="center"/>

p. After confirming the client's exercised share quantity, you may click "Complete Instruction Collection Early".

<img src="/assets/HLkEbDl7koicRCxAlOqjP65VpFg.png" src-width="2625" src-height="1439" align="center"/>

q. Then click "Submit Exercise Instructions".

Select whether to send a push notification to clients via the app.

<img src="/assets/BPPsbzzoKo741nxlY0kji51npog.png" src-width="2626" src-height="1450" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/U1MZbK8A9obsYdxtGv9j9DkkpAf.png" src-width="2372" src-height="1381" align="center"/>

r. Click "Backfill" to confirm the exercise quantities. You may choose non-original-value backfill or sub-account backfill and enter quantities, then click "Confirm".

Note: The backfill operation refers to the process of uploading selected client instructions to CCASS for processing and then backfilling the final CCASS processing results into the system.

<img src="/assets/WmMIbNYDzoY3CjxiYbNjPEQ8prh.png" src-width="2369" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/My5sbBEPAoX34DxY3VRjlBtEp3E.png" src-width="2372" src-height="1384" align="center"/>

s. After confirming the information is correct, click "Submit for Execution".

<img src="/assets/NmQibhf2AojpyvxesWdjZDLvpog.png" src-width="2869" src-height="1634" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/WONhbmhsRoRmpNxuVlnjk6uoptO.png" src-width="1420" src-height="1397" align="center"/>

t. Reviewers may click "Download Report SDR040-03", select the PDF file, and view the corporate action details under "Report Management &gt; Export List". If the information is correct, click "Execute Review".

<img src="/assets/Ha68b0hC8ogJQAxSDGojalbdprc.png" src-width="2621" src-height="1441" align="center"/>

u. After verifying the information, click "Approve". If selected, the app will send a push notification.

<img src="/assets/NCJab5YohoxyrgxXvlZjExAapEc.png" src-width="2639" src-height="1609" align="center"/>

v. The status will update to "Execution Successful".

<img src="/assets/VFSvbnluyoF7WHxujOojYes9pVf.png" src-width="2642" src-height="341" align="center"/>

x. To review distribution records, navigate to "Asset Account &gt; Account Details &gt; Stocks" to view the transaction history.

<img src="/assets/VqPWbcG12oPhjtx2KaTjX3pUplc.png" src-width="2627" src-height="558" align="center"/>
