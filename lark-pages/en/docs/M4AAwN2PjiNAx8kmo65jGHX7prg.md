# Overview

WTT provides real-time market data across multiple markets, including U.S. equities, Hong Kong stocks, Singapore, Shanghai and Shenzhen markets, and virtual assets; it also supports a stock screener feature that enables users to filter securities that meet specified criteria using multiple indicators.

# Main Features

- <b>Watchlist Page:</b> The stock watchlist page helps users manage and track stocks of interest, facilitating real-time market monitoring and keeping them informed of market developments.
- <b>Individual Stock Page:</b> The stock detail page provides intraday charts, K-line (candlestick) charts, and order book data, helping users conduct in-depth analysis of price movements and market trading activity to support decision-making.
- <b>Market Page:</b> Provides market-level data and analytical tools, including index constituents, market ranking, market anomalies, distribution of gains and losses, major indices, sector heat maps, and popular sectors.
- <b>Stock Screener:</b> Offers screening criteria such as universe scope, market data and valuation metrics, and financial data; users can filter stocks based on custom indicators to precisely identify investment opportunities.
- <b>Market Data Store:</b> Supports users applying for advanced data feeds, such as Hong Kong LV2, U.S. consolidated market data, and Nasdaq TotalView.
