# 9.0.1B Question: Why do listed private funds not display historical performance and unit net value?

<b>Answer:</b> Private funds do not have an automatic update mechanism. You must manually update the unit net value on the "Fund Management &gt; Private Funds &gt; Net Value Table" page (see Figure 9.0.1B-1); after updating, the historical records can be viewed in the App (see Figure 9.0.1B-2).

<img src="/assets/TjZpbe8mUouoIexzW0hjh9RDpjc.png" src-width="3782" src-height="2350" align="center"/>

# 9.0.1C Question: How are private funds displayed in the App?

<b>Answer:</b> In "Fund Management &gt; Private Funds &gt; App-side List", click "Create" to add the desired private funds.

<img src="/assets/JJyZbFRE9ooSA8xZF9Bj4afupTc.png" src-width="3782" src-height="2350" align="center"/>

# 9.0.1E Question: How do I resubmit the risk assessment questionnaire?

Answer: You can modify it in the backend or in the App.

1. In the backend, go to "KYC &gt; Risk Assessment Management" and click "Update Score" and "Update Documents" to modify the risk assessment level.

<img src="/assets/JawwbU8dhol8j6xLkx9jK2rNpwd.png" src-width="3782" src-height="2350" align="center"/>

1. In the App, go to "Portfolio &gt; More", tap "Risk Level", then tap "Retake" to modify the risk assessment.

<img src="/assets/Pi6QbblhyoSPTmxaJAYj0Ghyp1c.jpeg" src-width="1260" src-height="2736" align="center"/>
