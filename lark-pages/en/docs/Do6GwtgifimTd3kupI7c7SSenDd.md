# 15. Business Parameter Questions

# 15A Question: How do I create, edit, or freeze operator accounts and user roles?

<b>Answer:</b> (i) Click the "gear" icon in the upper-right corner of the system and select "Identity Management".

<img src="/assets/UKM6bXjU0oUmloxqOdQjLCb4phk.png" src-width="2636" src-height="719" align="center"/>

(ii) On the "Identity Management &gt; Personnel" page, click "Add Personnel".

<img src="/assets/XOJfbJk1go0ZDWxajGjjVZzdpfa.png" src-width="2621" src-height="858" align="center"/>

(iii) Fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required; the password can be set manually.

<div class="callout callout-bg-5 callout-border-5 callout-color-1">
<div class='callout-emoji'>üéÅ</div>
<p>Email addresses must be unique and cannot be reused.</p>
</div>

<img src="/assets/V1t4bb6c5orKVlxmMQwj7RfDpGd.png" src-width="2868" src-height="1633" align="center"/>

The "Personnel Type" can be set to Standard Administrator or Trader.

- Standard Administrator: may log in to the Securities Backend but may not log in to the WTT client.
- Trader: may log in to both the Securities Backend and the WTT client.

<img src="/assets/RtHIbIhOZoJCgAxVzl3jniZ4pVf.png" src-width="991" src-height="232" align="center"/>

(iv) In the action column, click "Edit" to modify the email and name;

click "..." to choose "Freeze" to freeze the account or "Mark as Resigned"; the status will be displayed as Deleted.

<img src="/assets/DVMrb1b9do76FYxQMcRj2QDLp7d.png" src-width="2239" src-height="481" align="center"/>

(v) To add permissions for a user: select "Individual Permissions" or "Role List," then click "Bind Permissions" or "Bind Role" to assign permissions.

<img src="/assets/Mbk8bKEubom7SNxel73j2qzEpLg.png" src-width="2610" src-height="1335" align="center"/>

(vi) When adding individual permissions, select from "Securities Backend / Client Management / Operations Backend / Global Settings / Securities Trading Frontend," check the relevant permissions, and then click "Confirm."

<div class="callout callout-bg-5 callout-border-5 callout-color-1">
<div class='callout-emoji'>üéÅ</div>
<p>Click &quot;Switch to Legacy Version&quot; to view a more detailed differentiation of the various permissions.</p>
</div>

<img src="/assets/SDxlb5sxeoH3d2xx15vjIZj2pGc.png" src-width="2624" src-height="1345" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/X8mfbP5ExoIbm4xyhYxj9AeqpZg.png" src-width="2108" src-height="1412" align="center"/>

# 15B Question: How do I modify a role's name or remarks?

<b>Answer:</b> On "Identity Management &gt; Roles," click "Edit." Toggle the "Hidden Information" button to reveal the fields where you can modify the role name and the remarks/description.
