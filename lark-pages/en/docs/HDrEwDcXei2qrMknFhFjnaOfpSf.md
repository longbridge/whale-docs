# 15.1 Approval Process Configuration

# 15.1 Approval Workflow Configuration

# 15.1A Q: How do I set the approvers for a work order?

<b>Answer:</b> Specify the relevant user account(s) as approvers. Please refer to the following steps.

(i). In the securities backend, click 'Identity Management' at the top right.

<img src="/assets/IVThb1OSaoSjZlxXMobj3V12pVf.png" src-width="2558" src-height="392" align="center"/>

(ii). On the 'Identity Management &gt; Approval Workflow Configuration' page, categorize work orders by business and click 'Configure'. Additionally, you can set the 'Default Approval Flow Configuration' to route work orders to designated default approvers.

<img src="/assets/McG9bICGIoPSXoxKbYUjVFE9pEh.png" src-width="2554" src-height="670" align="center"/>

(iii). Set the approval type and approvers: select 'Person' or 'Role'; multiple persons and roles are supported.

<img src="/assets/HzhWbeLaUoNifTxYKkojAfLKpGh.png" src-width="2546" src-height="900" align="center"/>

(iv). You can configure multi-level approval steps; after approval at one level, the process automatically advances to the next level until completion. You may also add 'CC' recipients.

Click 'Save' after completing the configuration.

<img src="/assets/CSRybGhaioasYlxDROgjoZ3WpXp.png" src-width="848" src-height="948" align="center"/>

# 15.1B Q: Why are approval work orders being routed to the super administrator account?

Answer: You need to set the approvers for work orders first. Click the gear icon at the top right of the system, then navigate to 'Identity Management &gt; Approval Workflow Configuration &gt; Default Approval Flow Configuration' to add approvers. After that, work orders will be routed to the designated users (see Figures 15.1B-1 and 15.1B-2).Approvers can be added by 'Role' or 'Person' account (see Figure 15.1B-3). After configuration is complete, you may submit new work orders to be processed by the newly assigned approver(s).
