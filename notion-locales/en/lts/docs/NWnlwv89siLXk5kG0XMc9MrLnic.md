# 6.0.1 Share/Dividend Distribution Operation (BE Category)

# 6.0.1 Share/Dividend Distribution Operations (BE Category)

a. On the "Corporate Actions" page, click "New" to add a corporate action.

<img src="/assets/MNOKbSLfAoXng5x2gOijPu60pBg.png" src-width="2628" src-height="1231" align="center"/>

b. Select Manual Create: choose Announcement Type (see 5.1 Announcement Types: BE (Benefit Entitlement) / Underlying (stock code)) / enter Announcement Number (must be unique) / select Ex‑Dividend Date or Record Date, then click "Confirm".

Quick Create: only applicable when a CCNAN05 file has been imported.

<img src="/assets/SSQiburobojdeSxGsG7jIVDPp3f.png" src-width="2246" src-height="1141" align="center"/>

c. After creation, the item will appear on the main interface. In the action column, click "Details" to enter the information.

<img src="/assets/M5MUbCtIHoZCCzx1LisjUz7pp0g.png" src-width="2089" src-height="885" align="center"/>

d. In the "Announcement Information" section, click "Edit"; fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required. After completing the entries, click "Confirm".

- Transfer Fee Charged: select "Yes"/"No".
- Distribution: typically NOMINEE.
- Execution Method: "Mandatory" cannot be chosen by the client; "Optional" can be selected by the client in the app.
- Payment Date: the system accounting date (viewable on the "Clearing Management &gt; End‑of‑Day Tasks" page; in the example below the accounting date is February 2).

<img src="/assets/OyWKbPHB6oDpNcxFAXfjQ5L5pKf.png" src-width="2300" src-height="635" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/A9dfbR4pqob1kmxPPcBjr8RrpNc.png" src-width="2166" src-height="1343" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/TmLabFYHioS446xPoeGjrfIqpAe.png" src-width="719" src-height="183" align="center"/>

e. In the "Scheme Information" section, click "Edit" to modify the dividend/distribution details.

<img src="/assets/AqXybhi0MoNOmix9gp9j8gxBpPd.png" src-width="2388" src-height="1412" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/S7ASbOH9oo2TbExDdRvj6WPVppe.png" src-width="1462" src-height="1372" align="center"/>

f. On the "Edit Scheme" page, fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required. The default scheme is "Yes". After entering the information, click "Confirm".

- Scheme Type: "BE-CASH DIVIDEND" indicates a cash dividend.
- Distribution Currency: the currency in which the distribution will be made.
- Dividend Rate: enter the amount distributed per share; for example, entering 0.78 = $0.78 dividend per share.

<img src="/assets/ARELbFS9woGTdRx8qSVjQs4Op1t.png" src-width="1585" src-height="1369" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Mgpkb9stvooBzgxCqrLjIkj1ppb.png" src-width="2387" src-height="1366" align="center"/>

g. Required fields for different BE announcement types are listed below:

(i). Scheme Type:
<b>BE - CASH DIVIDEND (Cash Dividend)</b>

- Distribution Currency
- Dividend Rate: amount distributed per share

<img src="/assets/NoO0bxj0Po12K8xXNCNjeNKBpze.png" src-width="472" src-height="342" align="center"/>

(ii). Scheme Type:

<b>BE - SCRIP DIVIDEND (Scrip Dividend - Optional)</b>

- Distribution Currency
- Dividend Rate: number of shares distributed per share
- Distribution Security (stock code)
- Reinvestment Price (price of distributed shares)

<img src="/assets/H9HWb0yGWo9q1oxQs8sjLJ7LpWd.png" src-width="474" src-height="341" align="center"/>

(iii). Scheme Type:

<b>BE - SCRIP EVENT (Scrip Event - Mandatory)</b>

- Distribution Security (stock code)

Example: 1 share for every 4 shares

- Ratio (Old): enter "4"
- Ratio (New): enter "1"

<img src="/assets/NQTYbfLwuoGlmRxa6brjuaUhpCc.png" src-width="470" src-height="341" align="center"/>

(iv). Scheme Type:

<b>BE - RIGHTS ISSUE (Rights Issue)</b>

- Distribution Security (stock code)
Example: 1 share for every 4 shares
- Ratio (Old): enter "4"
- Ratio (New): enter "1"

<img src="/assets/N88NbjstPozAYvxLttcjvZcepLf.png" src-width="474" src-height="338" align="center"/>

(v). Scheme Type:

<b>BE - BONUS ISSUE (Stock Dividend)</b>

- Distribution Security (stock code)

Example: 5 shares for every 2 shares

- Ratio (Old): enter "2"
- Ratio (New): enter "5"

<img src="/assets/VbvZbmRzboVXINxJkjxjzIaNpRg.png" src-width="471" src-height="344" align="center"/>

h. In the "Fee Rules" section, review the fees charged. To make a one‑time modification to the fees, click "Edit"; after completing the changes, click "Confirm".

<img src="/assets/QBmibemWEoYLgSxNUkaj3QwCpib.png" src-width="1407" src-height="1364" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/QxOabNT7Mo7GFSxG0bnj92tpppf.png" src-width="1580" src-height="1383" align="center"/>

i. If the Announcement Information, Scheme Information, and Fee Rules are correct, click "Register" and "Confirm" to submit the registration.

<img src="/assets/NfAibXyRhopW0CxklFBjrE19pjc.png" src-width="2385" src-height="1444" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/KXeFbRpnjohBb7xspn1jF9ohpWf.png" src-width="2364" src-height="1378" align="center"/>

j. After "Registration" succeeds, view the transferred amount in the "Details Summary" section. If the amount differs, click "Rounding Adjustment" to modify the distribution amount.

<img src="/assets/U6xnb6qBgope7ZxgdUbj5wkcpWh.png" src-width="2380" src-height="1396" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/AtRmbcCeloLhJQx31XBjuetupDe.png" src-width="513" src-height="357" align="center"/>

k. In the "Customer Details" section, view customers eligible for distribution.
Click "Add" to include individual customers in the dividend list;
click "Void" to remove a customer;
click "Details" to modify an individual customer's fee information or the distribution amount of their entitlement.

<img src="/assets/TOuAbn9XtoCiiTxsJU9jONw8p5E.png" src-width="2394" src-height="1404" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/Bhr9bYubNoDg4Zx5bErjrXD8pDh.png" src-width="1842" src-height="1393" align="center"/>

l. If any information has been modified, click "Re-register" to refresh the data. Once the information is confirmed correct, click "Submit for Execution" to proceed to the next step.

<img src="/assets/XlO7bvxluogHjuxBRKkjBCQXpyb.png" src-width="2367" src-height="1402" align="center"/>

m. After confirming the total dividend amount and total fees to be charged, click "Submit for Execution".

<img src="/assets/W1djb2BQnon8VsxsynNjqXgApQf.png" src-width="2391" src-height="1398" align="center"/>

n. The reviewer may click "Download Report SDR040‑09" and select the PDF file; view the corporate action details in "Report Management &gt; Export List". If the information is correct, click "Execute Review".

<img src="/assets/FekBbfGVQojp4AxHEvujVLyopSh.png" src-width="2644" src-height="1445" align="center"/>

<img src="/assets/JelVbffgro2yyfxzTCvjNBaPpl2.png" src-width="1903" src-height="1167" align="center"/>

o. Click "Approve". If this option is selected, the app will send a push notification.

<img src="/assets/PQFFbQK80ozsJixNSiRjHIRupGh.png" src-width="2390" src-height="1415" align="center"/>

p. The status will update to "Execution Successful".

<img src="/assets/Zm3nbhVaJozXFRxNmfdj3puEpSd.png" src-width="2639" src-height="625" align="center"/>

q. Locate the related distribution records and fees on the "Asset Accounts &gt; Account Details &gt; Cash" page.

<img src="/assets/CRjIbClohodaHuxAAKSjmkjdpxd.png" src-width="2639" src-height="1023" align="center"/>
