# Overview

The WTT Client List and Client Information components provide traders with one-stop access to detailed information on client assets, historical transactions, agency relationships, and account statements.

# Quick Start

Locate these two components in the WTT upper-right Components Toolbox under Client Categories; you can drag the components onto a panel or use them independently.
