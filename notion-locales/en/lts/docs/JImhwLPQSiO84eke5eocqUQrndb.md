# Quick Start

# Quick Start

# Overview

The Whale Help Center primarily supports the creation and management of various customer-facing article content, including help documents, frequently asked questions, market information, stock market content, event rules, and more. It also supports brokers in customizing article categories and maintaining article content.

# Prerequisites

You must obtain the following authorizations to use the system functions properly.

# Create an "Article Category"

Before creating articles, confirm whether the existing categories meet your requirements. If a new category scenario is needed, create an article category first.

<b>Path:</b> Customer Management System &gt; Help Center &gt; Article Categories

<b>Operation:</b> Click [Create Category] to add a new article category. Multi-level category creation is supported.

# Create an "Article"

The article list supports functions such as creating, editing, hiding/showing, and deleting documents.

<b>Path:</b> Customer Management System &gt; Help Center &gt; Articles

<b>Operation:</b> Click [Create Article] to edit the article's title, body content, external links, category, publishing scenarios, and other settings.
