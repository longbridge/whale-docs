# 6.1 Corporate Action Fees

# 6.1A Q: How do I set or modify the transfer fee for a corporate action?

A: On the "Corporate Actions &gt; Fee Management &gt; Global Rules" page, click "Transfer Fee Configuration".

<img src="/assets/EbXmb3DjCoY9T7xaXQbjFBKRpDd.png" src-width="2344" src-height="792" align="center"/>

# 6.1B Q: How do I configure a one-off corporate action so that no transfer fee is charged?

A: Follow the steps below.

(i) On the "Corporate Actions" page, click "Details".

<img src="/assets/Zy8UbBVlpo8sF5xtsFZjCbRmp4c.png" src-width="2850" src-height="1450" align="center"/>

(ii) In the "Announcement Information" field, click "Edit".

<img src="/assets/Zo2bbcWozoAffOxjlu7jKKVypxu.png" src-width="2870" src-height="1378" align="center"/>

(iii) Set "Collect Transfer Fee" to "Yes"/"No" to determine whether the fee is charged.

<img src="/assets/MQUtbq4s1om3XjxayHujX5AVpEb.png" src-width="2828" src-height="1442" align="center"/>

# 6.1C Q: How do I add or modify a handling fee for a corporate action?

A: Perform the following steps.

(i) On the "Corporate Actions &gt; Fee Management" page, click "Create/Edit".

<img src="/assets/II97bIF70otqLPxIDXwjN09Vpfd.png" src-width="2810" src-height="1434" align="center"/>

(ii) Complete the required fields marked with an asterisk

- Rate (not percentage): You may enter a fixed rate or charge as a percentage; for example, 0.25% = 0.0025.
- Charge Priority: Set the priority order for applying charges; typically enter 1.
- Rounding Method:
    - "Round Down" — round down, e.g., 6.9 → 6.0
    - "Round Up" — round up, e.g., 5.4 → 6.0

- Special Fee Constraints:
    - "No Limit" — the configured fee is charged regardless of the distributed amount.
    - "Individual Fee Not Exceeding Distributed Amount" — the charged fee will not exceed the distributed amount. Example: if the fee is $10 and the distributed interest is $5, only $5 will be charged; the fee will not exceed the distributed amount.

# 6.1E Q: For Hong Kong stocks distributing dividends in US dollars, how are handling fees calculated?

A: Handling fees follow the currency of the distribution. CCASS fees are charged in US dollars, so the system will collect those fees in US dollars; the transfer fee is charged by the Hong Kong transfer office and is billed in Hong Kong dollars by default.
